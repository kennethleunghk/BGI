Biopharmaceutical R&D

Analysis performed by research and development (R&D) teams is valuable proprietary knowledge. But managing such information across large teams is difficult.

With BGI Online’s ability to customise project permissions by granting specific access to individuals, team members can now focus on discovering the next medical breakthrough.

Security every step of the way

Data security is treated with utmost importace. Your data is encrypted at every step: during file transfer, while on pipeline analysis, and in storage.

BGI Online is compliant to HIPAA and the US-EU’s Data Protection Directive.

Easily share your results

You no longer need hard drives to download data and physically send to clients. BGI Online’s project settings allow you to share your results directly.
